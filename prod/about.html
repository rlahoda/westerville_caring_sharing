Who is Westerville Caring & Sharing?

Westerville Caring & Sharing (WC&S)became a 501(c)3 charitable organization in 1993. We are not associated with any organization or business. No part of any personal donation is used for any business-related expenses. All funding for these expenses are raised through our garage sales. Our mission is to encourage individuals, businesses, civic, church, and school groups in the Westerville area to care for the less fortunate and to share their resources of time, funds, and energy. We have been helping the Westerville community for 30 years and will continue our commitment to the Westerville community for many more years.
